Performances:

Name: Rachel Beverly

Description: Rachel Beverly is a Cortland-bred musician based in Ithaca, NY, who brings her own approach to a wide variety of cover songs, as well as original music. Rachel's pure, yet gutsy, vocals come from a heartfelt desire to bring her audience a fresh new twist to songs old and new. With the help of Dana Twigg and Marlette Sounds, Beverly released her very first EP on September 16th, 2016 titled, “From The Start”. A 4 track artistic journey, into the mind of what it means to be young and grasping the way of life. She attributes her success so far to the help and promotion of local artists and venues.

Time: 12:00pm Oct 1

Name: Sunny Weather

Description: Sunny Weather is a recent band that is a blend of roots rock and indigenous musics. Their live performances are known to be energetic and uplifting, leaving their audiences feeling invigorated and joyful. Sunny Weather's lyrics often reflect themes of nature, spirituality, and social justice, and their message resonates with fans of all ages and backgrounds. With their growing popularity and dedicated fan base, Sunny Weather is poised to become one of the most influential and beloved bands of their generation. They are often described as a zydeco band mixed with a little reggae, and their unique music style makes them a must see!

Time: 1:00pm Oct 1

Name: Firefly Jazz Quartet

Description: Firefly Trio with Diana Leigh plays a melodious mix of jazz standards, obscure gems, and some original tunes. Featuring the sweet vocal stylings of Ms. Leigh, their joyous jazz will lift your spirits and create a swinging soundscape for any situation. The Firefly Trio has been playing together for several years, and their chemistry on stage is undeniable. Their musical arrangements showcase the virtuosity of each member, with Diana Leigh's vocals serving as the perfect centerpiece. The group's dedication to their craft and passion for jazz is evident in every note they play, making them a favorite among jazz aficionados and casual listeners alike. Whether performing in intimate jazz clubs or on larger stages, the Firefly Trio with Diana Leigh is guaranteed to light up any room with their delightful music.

Time: 2:00pm Oct 1

Name: Janet Batch

Description: With her twangy voice and songs of working-class life, you might think that Janet Batch hails from the Blue Ridge Mountains or the hollers of Kentucky. But the Ithaca-based singer-songwriter actually grew up on a dairy farm in the small town of Litchfield, just southeast of Utica. Her unique sound is a fusion of Americana, folk, and indie rock, with introspective lyrics that touch on themes of love, loss, and the human experience. Batch's live performances are known for their intimate atmosphere and powerful emotional resonance, and she has gained a dedicated following among fans of indie and alternative music.

Time: 3:00pm Oct 1

Name: Leo + The Maydays

Description: Leo and the Maydays is an alt-pop band from Ithaca, NY known for their infectious melodies, dynamic live performances, and genre-bending sound. Led by frontman Leo Crandall, the band draws inspiration from a wide range of musical influences, including indie rock, pop, funk, and jazz, to create a unique and captivating sound that has earned them a dedicated following both locally and beyond. Their music is characterized by catchy hooks, intricate harmonies, and thoughtful lyrics that explore themes of love, loss, and personal growth

Time: 4:00pm Oct 1

Name: Neo Project

Description: NEO Project is a professional six piece, high energy dance band rooted in funky R&B grooves, Afro Cuban/Caribbean rhythms & jazzy, bluesy soul music. Hailing from New Orleans, Louisiana, NEO Project has become one of the most sought-after live acts in the region, known for their electrifying performances and tight musicianship. With a repertoire that spans decades of music history and an unwavering commitment to entertaining their audiences, NEO Project has established themselves as a top-tier live band, earning rave reviews from fans and critics alike.

Name: Ageless Jazz Band

Description: The Ageless Jazz Band is an 18-piece Big Band from Ithaca, NY that has been entertaining audiences for over 30 years with sophisticated charts from Swing to Bebop to Latin to Rock to Contemporary jazz. Under the direction of saxophonist and composer/arranger Peter Rothbart, the band features professional and semi-professional musicians in the area enthusiastically committed to making Central New York swing.

Time: 12:00pm Oct 2

Name: Yamatai

Description: Yamatai was founded in 2006 by Cornell undergraduates Haruki Yukawa ‘09 and Eva Kestner ’09. Eva served as Musical Director until 2009 while Haruki served as President until 2008. With the help of Masataka Kobayashi-sensei of Bonten (a professional taiko group from Japan), Yamatai was able to get started. Kobayashi-sensei brought drums from Japan, helped make sticks and stands, and held a master class for Yamatai's first generation. Yamatai has been playing Bonten pieces along with traditional taiko pieces ever since. More recently, Yamatai has incorporated original compositions into its repertoire.

Time: 1:00pm Oct 2

Name: Fall Creek Brass Band

Description: Ithaca’s favorite powerhouse brass band! FCBB fuses the sound of New Orleans street music with funk, jazz, pop, and rock-and-roll. Voted best local band in the 2020 Ithaca Times Readers Poll, FCBB performs original tunes, New Orleans classics, and brassy arrangements of your favorite pop songs. With a sizable collective of trumpets, trombones, saxes, drums, and a big green sousaphone, the Fall Creek Brass Band always attracts a crowd and gets everybody’s feet moving.

Name: Ariel Arbisser

Description: Whether on stage, in the studio, or working with students, singer/songwriter Ariel Arbisser leads with empathy.
