Interactive Cluster Map with Automatic Categories

A mapping tool that creates a map and marks every point of data from a data set. These points are automatically sorted by their given category and can be manually hidden/clustered with the layer control menu at the top right. The focus for this project was a proof of concept that a map representing thousands of data points while being user readible was possible. Demo

Research Project: Participation of team sports by high school students influence on underage drinking?

Was part of a research team that investigated the effect of high schoolers' regular participation in team sports on the probability of involvement in gambling-related activities. Through conducting various regressions on Stata, we found that the relationship between participation in sports and gambling-related activities was positive and significant. We utilized Stata with survey data from the High School Risk Survey from 2006. Final Report
